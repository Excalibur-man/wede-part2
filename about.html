About Aniwear

Our History

Aniwear is a Johannesburg-based clothing brand, specialising in clothing inspired from various anime(Japanese based cartoons). The business is owned and managed by an independent tailor who specialises in print ons or stitched in merchandised of high quality, whereas the tailor provides self-inspired merchandise from the various anime as mentioned this business is based in Johannesburg East which means all of it's clients reside on the Eastern side of Johannesburg. The brand has grown significantly due to use of social media, mainly Instagram, where it shows creative, original designs on the merchandise

Our Mission

To provide fairly priced and high quality merchandise, that is stylish and enhance the confidence of those who love anime and for them to express themselves in the most fashionable manner.

Our Vision

To become widely known in the streets of Johannesburg and expand all across the city and to become the most trusted brand in Johannesburg. To also have an overwhelming presence in social media not only on instagram but across all platforms.
